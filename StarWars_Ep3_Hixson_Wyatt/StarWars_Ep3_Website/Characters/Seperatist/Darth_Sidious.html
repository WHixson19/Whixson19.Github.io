Darth Sidious

Brief Biography

Darth Sidious, also known as Emperor Palpatine, was a Sith Lord who masterminded the downfall of the Jedi Order and the rise of the Galactic Empire. As Chancellor of the Republic, he manipulated events to engineer the Clone Wars and ultimately declared himself Emperor. His mastery of the dark side made him one of the most powerful Sith Lords in history.
