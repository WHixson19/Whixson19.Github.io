General Grievous

Brief Biography

General Grievous was a cyborg and the Supreme Commander of the Separatist Alliance military during the Clone Wars. Originally a warrior named Qymaen jai Sheelal, he was severely injured in a shuttle crash and was rebuilt by the Sith Lord Darth Sidious as a cybernetic being. Grievous was known for his fearsome combat abilities, particularly his mastery of lightsabers, which he collected from fallen Jedi. His ruthless tactics and skill made him a formidable foe in battle. Despite his mechanical body, Grievous retained a deep hatred for the Jedi Order and served as one of their greatest enemies.
