Count Dooku

Brief Biography

Count Dooku, also known as Darth Tyranus, was a former Jedi Master who turned to the dark side and became a Sith Lord. As the leader of the Separatist movement, he played a key role in the Clone Wars, training General Grievous and serving under Darth Sidious. His ambition ultimately led to his downfall at the hands of Anakin Skywalker.
